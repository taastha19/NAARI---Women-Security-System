FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Naari?

Naari is a comprehensive computerized women's security system designed to benefit the females of Banasthali,
including teenage students, working women, and homemakers. The system offers real-time location tracking,
emergency alerts, safety tips, educational resources, health information, community support, and confidentiality.

How can I use Naari?

You can sign up for Naari on our website and access the system through your account. You can use the
real-time location tracking feature, send emergency alerts, browse safety tips and educational resources,
and connect with the Naari community for support and assistance.

Is Naari free?

Yes,you can sign up for a free account and access basic features

How does Naari ensure my privacy?

Naari implements robust privacy and security measures to protect user data and ensure confidentiality.
We value your trust and are committed to keeping your information safe and secure.

Can I contact Naari support?

Yes, you can contact our support team through the Contact Us page on our website, or by emailing us at
[support@naari.com](mailto:support@naari.com). We are available to assist you with any questions or
concerns you may have.
