OUR VISION

Our vision is to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly computerized version of Women Security System
that will benefit the Females of the Banasthali including teenage students, Working Women as well as the
Home-Makers. Our system offers real-time location tracking, emergency alerts, safety tips and guidance,
educational resources, health information and support, mutual assistance, and confidentiality.

  • Emergency Alerts

  • Safety Tips and Guidance

  • Educational Resources

  • Health Information and Support

  • Mutual Assistance
