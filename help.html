HELP AND SUPPORT

Welcome to the Naari Help page, where you can find information and resources to assist you with using the Naari women's security system. If you need further assistance, please contact us through the form on the Contact Us page.

Getting Started

To get started with Naari, sign up for an account and download the Naari app from the App Store
or Google Play. Once you've installed the app, log in with your account details and follow the
on-screen instructions to set up your profile and preferences.

Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing issues with the Naari app or website, please try the following steps:

  • Check your internet connection

  • Restart the app or your device

  • Update the Naari app to the latest version

  • Clear your browser cache

  • Contact Naari support for further assistance

Contact Naari Support

If you need additional help or have questions about Naari, please contact us using the form on the Contact Us page. We'll do our best to respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.
