Stay Safe with Naari

At Naari, your safety is our top priority. We have compiled a list of safety tips for you to follow, whether you're walking alone at night, using public transportation, or just going about your daily routine. Our goal is to empower you with the knowledge you need to stay safe and secure.

General Safety Tips

  • Trust your instincts. If something doesn't feel right, it probably isn't.

  • Stay aware of your surroundings. Avoid distractions like headphones and mobile devices.

  • Carry a charged mobile phone with emergency contact numbers programmed in.

  • Walk with confidence. Keep your head up and make eye contact with people around you.

  • Choose well-lit, populated routes when walking alone at night.

  • Avoid using ATMs after dark or in isolated areas.

  • Carry pepper spray or another personal safety device for protection.

Public Transportation Safety Tips

  • Plan your route in advance and use busy, well-lit stations whenever possible.

  • Avoid isolated or deserted train cars or buses.

  • Sit near other people or near the driver if possible.

  • Keep your belongings close to you and avoid displaying expensive items.

  • If you feel threatened, move to a safer location and alert the driver or other authority figures.

Travel Safety Tips

  • Research your destination and learn about local customs and laws.

  • Register with your embassy or consulate before you travel.

  • Keep your passport, money, and other valuables in a secure location.

  • Avoid traveling alone in unfamiliar areas, especially at night.

  • Be cautious when accepting rides or invitations from strangers.

  • Use reputable transportation services and avoid hitchhiking.

  • Stay in touch with family or friends and let them know your itinerary and whereabouts.
